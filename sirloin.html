Sirloin Escargot

Price: R 280

Serving Size: 1(300g)

Gently roasted pasture-raised juicy jump steak, well seasoned, cut into strips, with a dollop of taste enhancing Beurre d'Escargot, and a side of roasted vegetables.
The Beurre d'Escargot is a specialty of our head chef and is our home-made ghee mixed with fresh parsley. This type of butter is traditionally served in France with snails (escargot) hence the name.
